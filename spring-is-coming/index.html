A friendship?

poem

By NOOR In 9 min read

A friendship?

When did I cheat you? I seem to forget
a new hope of light was visible when we first met.
We talked day and night
did not care if it was dull or bright.
We became great friends
fortune lines were similar on our hands.
Stupid arguments was our speciality
I thought of you as smart and you thought of me as witty.
We used to argue a lot
oh we were sailing in the same boat.
We used to share all our experiences
you sent me hearts i sent you kisses.
Then something happened
I did not know how to mend.
It was not all of a sudden
it was like hell after heaven.
Arguments transformed into fights
our talks were not like before, light.
As you got disappointed I shed a tear
it was so much pain i could not bear.
A few strings in my heart started to break
I wanted it all to be a dream I would laugh at when I wake.
But it was all reality I had to face
it all happened so fast that I could not keep pace.
I do not know if you are that much hurt
but I am, everything is totally absurd.
What have you done to me?
I have become unstable,
changes you caused are now noticeable.
Still I tried to pull things together
but all efforts went in vain, friendship got worse instead of getting better.
I do not know what is wrong
what is it that is keeping you away for so long.
Then you had a lover, you were back to normal
but soon I found you were not normal but formal.
As if you kept me at a distance
you thought you could balance.
I tried to adjust
keeping us together was a must.
And then you became rude
you became selective and talked only when in mood.
I tried to understand this
oh but you said- ignorance is bliss.
Last night I tried again
again all went in vain.
You started you accusation
it was spoiling our relation.
I tried to control and I apologized
but till then you were agonized.
You abused my tears, called me a bitch
how could you think i would ever ditch?
You think I can never comprehend
but I valued you my friend.
Now it is too late, our friendship cannot be revived;
you moved on and I sighed.

-NOOR
